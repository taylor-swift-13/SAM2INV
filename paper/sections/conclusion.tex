\section{Conclusion}\label{sec:conclusion}
%% ============================================================

We presented \sam{}, a system for certified numerical loop
invariant generation comprising three contributions:
\lf{} for scalable training data synthesis;
a multi-stage sampling-and-filtering pipeline producing formally
verified, non-trivial invariants;
and an offline distillation strategy that embeds verifier feedback
into SFT\,+\,DPO training data via reverse CoT synthesis and
on-policy augmentation---with no human annotation or online RL.

Future work includes systematic fine-tuning experiments over
successive training rounds, extension to heap-manipulating programs
with separation-logic invariants, and integration of symbolic
techniques (abstract interpretation, interpolation) for a hybrid
symbolic--neural architecture.
